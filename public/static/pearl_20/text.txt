Pearl is the annual national
cultural fest of BITS Pilani,
Hyderabad Campus. Incepted in
2009 as an intra-college festival,
the fest grew into a national level
college cul tural fest with
participation from more than
100 colleges across India. Pearl
hosts a variety of cultural events
and competitions which include
music, dance, literary,
photography and quizzing.
